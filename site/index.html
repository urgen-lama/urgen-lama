Guitar is a very interesting instrument. It consists of six strings. The most amazing thing about guitar is that you can add many variations with the help of techniques like vibrato, bending, pullups, and hammering. Guitar has been a staple instrument, because it can produce different emotions. Many great players like Jimi Hendrix, Jimmy Page, John Frusciante and many more had played iconic solos and produced unforgettable tone that anyone can enjoy. Guitar is an iconic instrument and it has played very important part in the development of music.

Music production is the process where many instruments and sounds are recorded and put together using DAW. There are many famous DAWs like FL Studio, Ableton Live, Logic Pro and many more. Nowadays, many famous songs are produced by using only laptop. With the development of powerful chips, producers can now produce songs whenever they go.
